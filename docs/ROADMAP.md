# Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap provides a high-level view of the Engineering OS journey.

Its purpose is to answer one question:

> **"Where am I in my engineering journey?"**

Detailed learning objectives, projects, interview preparation, and completion criteria are maintained separately in `CURRICULUM.md`.
